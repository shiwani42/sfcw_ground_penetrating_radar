# Project Milestone Reports

Attached are the reports of several milestones of the project:

---

### 🧩 Milestones 0 and 1

These were aimed at gaining a thorough understanding of the entire system, including:

- Principle of operation  
- Block and circuit diagrams  
- Risk analysis  
- Project planning  

---

### ⚙️ Milestone 2 — Simulation

This phase focused on simulating various blocks of the system. Simulations were carried out for major components using:

- **SPICE** for LF board  
- **MATLAB** for principle of operation  
- **GNU Radio** for RF board (attempted)  
- **Ansys HFSS** for antenna simulation  

> Simulations were critical, as components in the BOM were project-specific and not generally available in the lab. Additionally, the RF board couldn't be tested on a breadboard even if the components had arrived.

⚠️ However, **circuit-level simulation** of the RF board was not properly completed due to lack of knowledge and access to required simulators. This caused significant setbacks in the later stages of the project and should be taken seriously in future iterations.

---

### 🖥️ Milestone 3 — PCB and CAD Design

This phase focused on:

- Designing the PCB  
- Creating CAD models

Special attention was given to **RF PCB design**, which involves multiple considerations to follow **good RF practices**.

---

### ✅ Milestone 4 — Final Demo

The project concluded with the final demonstration of the system.
