# 🧩 PCB Design Overview

## 📦 Part-A: LF and RF Boards

For **Part-A**, two separate PCBs were designed:

- **LF Board**:  
  Contains components operating at low frequencies.  
  It processes the received signal after mixing with the transmitted signal.

- **RF Board**:  
  Comprises components that operate at RF frequencies.

**KiCAD** was used for designing both PCBs.

---

## 🚫 Part-B: No Dedicated PCB

For **Part-B**, no dedicated PCB was developed, as the system involved minimal analog or digital logic.
